创客嘉年华的公告栏

2015年创客嘉年华MakerCarnival全面升级为壹零科技节 10 Festival

【活动回顾】
2012.9－2012.10月：北京国际设计周
2012.4－2012.5月：创客嘉年华
2011.8－2011.10月：北欧艺术节
2013.9-2013.10月：2013北京创客嘉年华
2014.10：2014北京创客嘉年华

【简介 INTRODUCTION】
壹零科技节是中国第一个自发主办、最具创新及国际视野的大型全民科技互动节日。是科技与教育，科技与娱乐，科技与艺术的全面升级，实现产业、品牌各领域的跨界结合。在这里，公众将体验科技互动产品、接触各种新奇好玩的智能硬件，感受科技跨界所带来的惊喜。这不仅是中国第一个全民科技生活方式的节日，更是中国最大的科技互动、交互娱乐的盛会。

【INTRODUCTION】
10-FESTIVAL is the most creative large-scale technical interaction festival for all and the first spontaneous host of its kind in China. It combines fully upgrades of technology with education, entertainment and art, realizing the cross-boundary union of industry and brands. In 10-FESTIVAL, people will experience maker products and diverse smart hardware, enjoying the cross-border collision of technology and lifestyle. It is not only the first festival of technology lifestyle among the whole people, but also the greatest gala of interactive technology and entertainment in China.
