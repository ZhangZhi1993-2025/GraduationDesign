MEETUP创客聚会




近期活动时间表

时间：每周四 晚上7:30 地点：北京创客空间（中关村梦想实验室四层，查看地图）

Meetup是创客空间会员们和爱好者们线下交流互动的活动。每人有3-5分钟时间介绍自己的项目或者自己想做的内容。可以通过交流讨论组团队完成项目。所有人都可以免费参加。

Meetup in Beijing Maker Space is a kind of activity specifically for the makers here to interact and communicate with each other offline. It has been held at 7:30 p.m. on every Thursday since November 2013.

In Meetup, everyone is given 3-5 minutes to show their projects which are in progress or finished. The participants can easily discuss the ideas they interested in and make comments as well as creative suggestions. Furthermore, they can find new fellows from Meetup to cooperate.

EVENT DETAIL

Organizer: Beijing Makerspace & Members
Time: Every Thursday Night, 7:30 PM
Place: Beijing Makerspace
Fees: Free
